Apple Gross Merchandise Value 2015 to 2018

Here we have E-commerce GMV (Gross Merchandice Value) data collected by Yippit, which is a third party data agency that focuses on providing online platform statistics. Specifically, in this case, the viewer is able to visualize the trend of change of Apple GMV (sales) on Alibaba E-commerce platfrom from October, 2015 to April, 2020. The visualization of E-commerce data helps the viewer to view the progression of change of the GMV data through chronological lenses.
